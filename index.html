Privacy Policy

Risk Vector Tools LLC
Effective date: April 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to all applications published by Risk Vector Tools LLC, including Lantern: KJV Bible & AI Study.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our services. This includes your email address when you create an account, and app usage data such as bookmarks, highlights, notes, and reading progress that you create within the app.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to provide and improve our apps. We use Supabase to securely store your account data and sync it across your devices. We never sell your personal data to third parties.

AI Features

Our apps include AI-powered features that process text you provide (such as search queries or journal entries). This text is sent to our secure backend and processed using the Anthropic API. We do not store your AI query content beyond what is necessary to provide the service.

In-App Purchases

Premium subscriptions are processed through Google Play. We do not collect or store your payment information. Subscription management is handled through RevenueCat.

Advertising

Our apps contain no advertisements. We do not share your data with advertising networks.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including SSL/TLS encryption and row-level security on all user data.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time from within the app under Settings → Delete Account.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
